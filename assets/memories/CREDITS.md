# Asset credits

All bundled media is royalty-free / public-domain-equivalent and used here only
as stand-ins for what would, in production, be a customer's digitized family
media. Titles and dates in `memories.json` are fictional framing for the demo.

## Video
- `beach-day-2008.mp4`, `camping-1996.mp4` — generic sample clips from
  [samplelib.com](https://download.samplelib.com) (free sample media),
  trimmed to <30s and re-encoded to 720p H.264 / AAC for broad device support.

## Photos
- `grandma-80th.jpg`, `first-birthday.jpg`, `family-reunion.jpg` — from
  [Lorem Picsum](https://picsum.photos) (Unsplash-sourced, free to use),
  downscaled to a 1400px long edge.
